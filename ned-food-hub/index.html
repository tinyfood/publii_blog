Ned Food Hub

By Greg Willson
Published on June 16, 2019

My goal for this hub is to network with some of my friends and neighbors who have expressed interest in high quality food access, and willing to work together.

To me, 'high quality food access' optimizes locality, freshness, seasonality, healthy diets and mindbendingly delicious recipies that inspire us to reach out more to one another, while creating healthy containers for positive interaction with other members of the general community.

This food hub is live as of this writing:

https://nedfoodhub.herokuapp.com

  I am currenty working on adding some of my products. 

 

Greg Willson

Greg has worked with technology and community networks since the mid 90s He is the contact person for Tiny Food and available to assist leaders of small groups in making use of the proposed food hub system, or listen to ideas on how it could serve your community better.
