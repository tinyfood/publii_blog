New community hubs forming

By Greg Willson
Published on June 16, 2019

Ned Food Hub  live demo site

 Ned food hub is currently directed at a small group of interested locals (both farmers and health food shoppers) who would like to collaborate on purchases from local farmers and some of the Boulder and Nederland food markets. We are testing the app for finer details, and inviting interested users to try it out.  details

Tiny Food Canton  live demo site

Upstate New York group forming in the Canton/ Potsdam region forming to support better representation of local farmers and cooperation between health food bulk staples shoppers who venture to Trader Joes, Costco, or other regional health food stores with products that are specifically difficult to find locally, or priced significantly better further away. details

Mamawatoto Womens' Group Shop, Machakos, Kenya  live demo site

Claudia Mbibda Mwende has been working in her home community of Machakos, Kenya, located halfway between the busy port city of Mambasa and Kenya's inland capitol, Nairobi. She has worked with groups to develop new products from waste stream materials sources, in support of job creation, zero waste initiatives, gardens and nurseries.  Tiny Food proudly works to support Claudia's work and that of the groups she works with.
