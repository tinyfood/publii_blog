So if OFN Exists, why Tiny Food?

Tiny food is built on software that is over 10 years more modern than the core CMS being used for the OFN platform.   TF is working to transition new users with the flexibility of Saleor software platform, and has have good success creating new instances for new user groups and use cases on heroku, free of charge.  This low bar for participation excites the team as we see new relevancy in the states, Kenya and beyond.

 

 

We are a network which supports new users by allowing them to easily create their own 'nodes' on the network, or test instances of Tiny Food's underlying software .  Nodes can be kept private, or used collectively by a small group.  A small group of community members who have an interest in seeing a more vibrant local food economy can come together by creating a new instance and making use of the secure app provided by heroku . (ie. [your food hub name].herokuapp.com

Simplicity and Freedom

You are free to download and change our software to your own needs as per this license. You are encouraged, but not required to share special customizations back to the network.
