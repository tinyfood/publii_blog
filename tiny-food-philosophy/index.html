Tiny Food Philosophy

By Greg Willson
Published on June 16, 2019

We are a network which supports new users by allowing them to easily create their own 'nodes' on the network, or test instances of Tiny Food's underlying software .  Nodes can be kept private, or used collectively by a small group.  A small group of community members who have an interest in seeing a more vibrant local food economy can come together by creating a new instance and making use of the secure app provided by heroku . (ie. [your food hub].herokuapp.com

Simplicity and Freedom

You are free to download and change our software to your own needs as per this license. You are encouraged, but not required to share special customizations back to the network.
