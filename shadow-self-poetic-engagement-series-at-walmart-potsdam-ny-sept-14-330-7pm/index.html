Shadow Self Poetic Engagement Series at Walmart, Potsdam NY Sept 14 3:30-7PM

by Greg Willson
published on August 21, 2019

TinyFood and SCD Hub have started a series of 13 events designed to help build bridges between different communities within modern American culture.  Our event is designed to be inclusive, respectful, and an exploration into the ways we define community, and the 'shadow aspects' of our modern lives and behaviors.  We all have basic needs and common ground that we share regardless of where we live and how we live our lives.  The biggest challenge sometimes can be to accept the uncomfortable, unjust, and terrible things in life, so that common ground..

SCD Hub's "Shadow Self Poetic Engagement Series" will support all people who wish to come by Walmart on Sept 14 2019, between 3:30 PM and 7 PM with artists Ǥœg Willson assisting young and old participants to share their thoughts and ideas on shadow self concepts. Claudia Mwende Mbinda will be participating remotely from Njiru, Kenya where she lives and works to develop community projects related to recycled materials, medicinal gardens, poetics, and acrobatics. 

This event supports the diverse community of people who we are at this time, with no judgement. We have agreed to Walmart's 'Informational Content' terms: "You may not communicate verbally or in writing any message on Company property that contains or depicts violence, obseneties, pornography, gross or gruesome scenes, racial or ethinic or inflammatory slogans likely to provoke a disturbance. This restrictions applies to all forms of oral communications, body gestures, signs, posters, placards, handbills, or written materials."
